1. 📰 **Summary:**  Operation RapTor, a multinational law enforcement operation, resulted in the arrest of 270 individuals across ten countries.  These arrests targeted both vendors and buyers operating on the dark web.

2. ⚠️ **Impact:** The operation significantly disrupted dark web marketplaces dealing in illegal goods and services.  It demonstrates the increasing international cooperation and capabilities in investigating and prosecuting dark web crime.  The arrests serve as a deterrent to others involved in similar activities.

3. ✅ **Takeaways:**  This large-scale operation highlights the vulnerability of dark web users and vendors.  International collaboration is crucial in combating cybercrime and maintaining online safety.  Law enforcement agencies are actively pursuing and prosecuting individuals involved in dark web illegal activities.
